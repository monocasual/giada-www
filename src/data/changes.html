This is the second bugfix release in the 1.4 series which makes working with scenes smoother and more flexible, fixes a few annoyances, and brings some welcome cleanup under the hood.

  • Add ability to copy the entire scene to another;
  • Add ability to force-change a scene;
  • Add ability to clear all actions in current scene or in all scenes;
  • Add ability to free all sample channels in current scene or in all scenes;
  • Add missing pop-over labels to scene buttons;
  • Fix sample channels being killed abruptly on scene change;
  • Minor refactoring and code cleanup.
